# Reports

Research and insight reports — same Elixirr Digital branding and site structure. Each report is self-contained with its own sections and sidebar.

## Key takeaways

- AI-assisted development works best when human merge accountability remains explicit.
- PR review quality is a throughput and cognitive-load problem, not just a tooling problem.
- Governance controls (data handling, security checks, auditability) need to be designed into delivery workflows, not bolted on.
- Smaller, intent-rich PRs consistently improve review speed, depth, and reliability.
- Most reports include operational templates and phased rollout plans, not only theory.
