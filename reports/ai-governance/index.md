Research and templates for **safely using AI tools** in enterprise WordPress and Laravel client projects. Covers data leakage prevention, PII redaction, on-prem vs SaaS, client approval workflows, GDPR-oriented compliance, and secure coding validation.

## Deliverables

| Document | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| [1. Governance Framework Draft](01-governance-framework-draft) | Principles, DLP, PII redaction, deployment model, approval workflows, GDPR considerations, secure coding, roles. |
| [2. Risk Register Template](02-risk-register-template) | AI risk register with example risks, control measures, assessment lenses, and GRC integration. |
| [3. Recommended Tooling Stack](03-tooling-stack) | DLP, PII redaction, on-prem/SaaS, approval workflows, compliance, and secure-coding tooling with evaluation checklist. |

## Topics Covered

1. **Data leakage prevention** — Session data, training data, model IP; preventative and detective controls.
2. **PII redaction pipelines** — When and how to redact; pipeline design; policy rules.
3. **On-prem vs SaaS** — Decision framework and hybrid approach for client work.
4. **Client approval workflows** — Risk-based tiers (1/2/3) and approval components.
5. **Compliance (GDPR context)** — Legal basis, obligations, anonymity, DPIA.
6. **Secure coding validation via AI** — Approved SAST/SCA and safeguards.

*Adapt to your jurisdiction and client contracts; seek legal and compliance sign-off before adoption.*
