This report is a **research analysis** of techniques described in [Jaymin West's *Agentic Engineering Book*](https://jayminwest.com/agentic-engineering-book), assessed against the existing Dark Factory multi-agent research workflow. It identifies what the workflow already does well, where the highest-impact gaps lie, and provides a prioritised action plan for improvement.
